---
title: Payment Failed Error
description: Solutions for payment failure issues.
---

## Symptoms
- Customer payment does not go through
- Error: "Payment failed"

## Possible causes
- Card information incorrect
- Insufficient funds
- Payment gateway temporary outage

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify card details">
    Ask the customer to re-enter card number, expiry, CVV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another card">
    Suggest using a different payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait and retry">
    Gateway issues may resolve in a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
