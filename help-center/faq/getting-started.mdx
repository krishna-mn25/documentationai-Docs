## Overview

Sign up and create your organization to begin using Monetizenow.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://dashboard.monetizenow.com/signup`
2. Enter your email and company details
3. Confirm your email address
4. Log in and set up your first product

## Need help?
Contact support if you have trouble registering.
