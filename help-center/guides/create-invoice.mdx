---
title: How to Create an Invoice
description: Simple guide to creating your first invoice.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Dashboard">
    Go to `https://dashboard.monetizenow.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Invoices">
    Click "Invoices" in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Invoice">
    Click the "Create Invoice" button and enter necessary details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invoice">
    Review and click "Send" to email your customer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
