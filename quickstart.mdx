## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account registration
- Access to your company details (name, address) for initial setup
- Browser support: Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (latest versions)

</Callout>

Monetizenow makes B2B commerce frictionless. Follow these steps to create your account, configure the dashboard, build your first quote with Quote Builder, and generate a bill in Billing Universe.

## Step 1: Sign Up and Log In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.monetizenow.io/signup` and enter your email, company name, and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Navigate to `https://app.monetizenow.io/login`. Use your credentials to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Initial Dashboard Setup

After logging in, complete the quick onboarding:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Products" icon="package">
    Go to the PIM section. Click "Add Product" and enter details like name (`Premium Plan`), price (`$99/month`), and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect CRM" icon="link">
    In Settings > Integrations, select your CRM (e.g., Salesforce) and authorize the connection to sync customer data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Billing Preferences" icon="settings">
    Under Billing > Preferences, configure currency (`USD`) and payment terms (`Net 30`).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Quote

Use Quote Builder for guided selling. Start by selecting a customer and product.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="UI Method" icon="monitor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Quote" icon="plus">
        Dashboard > Quote Builder > "Create Quote". Select customer and add `Premium Plan`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize" icon="edit-3">
        Apply 10% discount and set ramp (3 months at 50%). Preview and send.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Method" icon="code">
    Use the Quotes API to create programmatically.

    <CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript,Python">
      ````bash
      curl -X POST https://api.monetizenow.io/v1/quotes \
        -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
        -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
        -d '{
          "customer_id": "cust_123",
          "items": [{"product_id": "prod_premium", "quantity": 1}]
        }'
      ````

      ````javascript
      const response = await fetch('https://api.monetizenow.io/v1/quotes', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: {
          'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
          'Content-Type': 'application/json'
        },
        body: JSON.stringify({
          customer_id: 'cust_123',
          items: [{ product_id: 'prod_premium', quantity: 1 }]
        })
      });
      ````

      ````python
      import requests
      response = requests.post(
        'https://api.monetizenow.io/v1/quotes',
        headers={
          'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
          'Content-Type': 'application/json'
        },
        json={
          'customer_id': 'cust_123',
          'items': [{'product_id': 'prod_premium', 'quantity': 1}]
        }
      )
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Generate Your First Bill

In Billing Universe, bills handle subscriptions, usage, and more.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Bill" icon="file-text">
    Billing > "Generate Bill" from your quote. Select period and meter usage if applicable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Payment" icon="credit-card">
    Apply payment or schedule invoice. Track in the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Monitor the Revenue Lifecycle dashboard for quote-to-bill progression. Integrate usage metering for metered billing.
</Callout>
